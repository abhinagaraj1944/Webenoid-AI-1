============================================
   WEBENOID AI - Excel Add-in Setup Guide
============================================

STEPS TO INSTALL (ONE TIME ONLY):

Step 1: Extract this ZIP folder anywhere on your PC
        Example: C:\WebenoidAddin

Step 2: Double-click "Install-WebenoidAI.bat"
        (Click Yes if Windows asks for permission)

Step 3: Close ALL Excel windows completely

Step 4: Reopen Excel

Step 5: Click Insert > My Add-ins

Step 6: Click "MY ADD-INS" tab

Step 7: Click "Webenoid AI" and press Add

============================================
   DONE! The Webenoid AI panel will open
   on the right side of Excel.
============================================

TO UNINSTALL:
   Double-click "Uninstall-WebenoidAI.bat"

NOTE: Requires active internet connection.
      The Webenoid server must be online.
============================================
